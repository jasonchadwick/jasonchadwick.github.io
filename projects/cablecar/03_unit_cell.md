Figure 3. The tiled unit cell architecture considered in this work. On the left is a closeup of one unit cell, which has (a) one interaction zone where gates are performed, (b) one readout port, where a qubit can be measured, and (c) shuttling channels, where qubits can travel within and between unit cells. (d) shows a simplified version of the unit cell, and (e) shows how the unit cells can be tiled into a grid-like arrangement.
